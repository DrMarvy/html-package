Being a driven and dynamic individual, with a penchant for learning and personal development, I have cultivated aptitudes and competencies beyond by foundational degree. My expertise extends beyond Pharmacy as I have developed competencies in retail management, supply chain management, research, academic editing, and creative writing. I am now learning to write code in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React, in hopes of becoming a web developer.

I am driven by a passion for improving healthcare outcomes through continuous learning and innovation. With excellent communication skills and the ability to collaborate effectively with multidisplinary teams. I am confident in my ability to make valuable contributions wherever I go.
